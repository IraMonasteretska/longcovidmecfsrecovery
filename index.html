Hi I’m Felicity Read MY JOURNEY WITH ME/CFS

Back in 2017 a perfect storm of stressors culminated in a climactic collapse of my health. The stressors were:

  • Viruses, herpes simplex (coldsores), respiratory virus, herpes zoster (shingles).
  • Hep B/HepC vaccination required for nursing overseas.
  • Loss of several people I cared about in tragic circumstances in a short space of time.
  • Workplace stress combined with shift work,
    and much more.

I started getting gut issues, fatigue, headaches, and a feeling of chronic anxiety and emotional fragility. I couldn't concentrate. I thought I had 'burnout' from my job as a Registered Nurse. Sadly I had to leave my job as I was too unwell to work. It took 2 years of medical investigations, therapies and supplements which cost $1000's with little results to finally get a diagnosis of ME/CFS from a Neurologist. By this stage I had experienced several major crashes and now had many other symptoms and sensitivities.

Being a nurse who had worked extensively in Palliative Care where quality of life and self-care were paramount, I thought I knew how to improve my quality of life and get well again.
I tried unsuccessfully to heal. Why wasn't I successful?
Well I wasn't addressing the real ROOT causes that got my health into this dysfunctional state. There were many physiological, emotional, spiritual root causes that I wasn't aware of at that time that were preventing full recovery.

I am now fully recovered living my life even better than before ME/CFS.
I walk Orewa Beach New Zealand where I live every morning at sunrise with my dogs Alfie and AJ. I love being in nature, photography, attending Latin/Ballroom dance classes weekly. I get to enjoy being an active mother, grandmother, sister, partner and friend again. I travel internationally and enjoy adventures along the way. The biggest shift for me is a new appreciation of everything in life. I love, feel joy, and laugh way more, and at a deeper level than before ME/CFS. You too can get your life back better than before!
